Hi, I'm Michael Van Le!

Throughout my life, I have always had a passion for tinkering and figuring things out. This curiosity has lead me to coding and the endless possibilities it brings with it. After joining General Assembly's software engineering immersive, and working with a wide array of developers, I've come to realize that one of the best parts of being in this field is how rewarding the communal and team based community is.

Coming from a background in public service, where I worked with the low income community as a government employee, I have assisted diverse populations from all walks of life. I pride myself on my ability to listen, learn, and disseminate complex data into easily digestible pieces. With the skills gained through GA and my past experience I'm excited to continue my adventure and grow further as part of a team.
